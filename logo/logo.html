The Logic

Weaverbirds build intricate, strong nests by weaving together individual strands. This is a perfect metaphor for Codeunia. The two "wings" of the logo are formed from the universal code brackets (`< >`), representing the individual developers and technologies.

Where they meet, they form a strong, unified structure. The story is clear and powerful: **Codeunia is where individual strands of code and talent are woven together to build something strong, beautiful, and communal.**
